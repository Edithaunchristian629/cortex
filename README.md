# Cortex

A companion with the brain you're supposed to have.

---

Cortex is a personal AI companion that absorbs any signal from your life — health, finances, mood, diet, calendar, social interactions, habits, whatever you feed it — finds the patterns you can't see from inside your own experience, and tells you what it sees. Whether you want to hear it or not.

It's not a health tracker. Not a habit app. Not a wellness platform. Not a finance tool. It's the brain that remembers everything, connects what you do to what you feel, and doesn't pretend the connection isn't there when the data is obvious.

**Read the full idea: [CORTEX.md](CORTEX.md)**

---

## Quick overview

- **Input is anything** — voice, text, photos. Log a meal, a mood, a decision, a conversation. Connect data sources via MCP. If you have a wearable, health data flows in automatically.
- **Finds cross-domain patterns** — stress-triggered spending, person-to-headache correlations, diet-to-focus links, sleep-to-decision-quality loops. The graph doesn't care what domain the signal comes from.
- **Honest by default** — never lies to make you feel better. Three certainty modes: direct, evidenced, or honest uncertainty. Never fakes either.
- **Speaks when it matters** — respects your silence preferences but overrides when the pattern is clear and you're not seeing it.
- **Runs on local models** — Gemma, Llama, Mistral. Your data never leaves your machine. Privacy is structural, not policy.
- **Open-ended goals** — "stop impulse spending," "sleep better," "feel more alive." Cortex anchors your intent and watches reality against it.
